READING
Text 1
This passage is about recycling in cities.
Many cities around the world are trying to reduce waste. Recycling programs allow residents to separate paper, plastic, and glass so these materials can be reused instead of thrown away. Studies show that cities with strong recycling programs produce less garbage and save money on waste disposal.
1. What is the passage mostly about?
A. how to start a business
B. recycling programs in cities
C. the history of garbage
D. how to save money at home
Answer: B. recycling programs in cities
2. According to the passage, what is one benefit of recycling?
A. it makes cities larger
B. it produces more garbage
C. it saves money on waste disposal
D. it increases pollution
Answer: C. it saves money on waste disposal
